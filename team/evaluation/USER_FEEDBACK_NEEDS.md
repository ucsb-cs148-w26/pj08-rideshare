
# User Feedback Needs (Inter-Team Evaluation)

**Date:** 2/25

**Purpose:** Help us identify confusing areas and improve user value in the UCSB Rideshare App. Please give honest, actionable feedback!

---

## Key Tasks for Evaluators

1. Register an account
2. Browse available rides and join one
3. Host a ride

---

## What We Want to Learn

- Is anything confusing or unclear during registration, joining, or hosting a ride?
- Is pricing and payment info understandable, even though payment is not fully integrated?
- What kind of notifications would you want from the app?

---

## Specific Metrics & Questions

### Registration & Joining

- Rate how easy it was to register (1-5)
- Rate how clear it was to browse and join a ride (1-5)
- Rate how clear the price was for each ride (1-5)
- Did you understand the price for each ride? (Yes/No + explain)
- What info was missing or confusing on the ride card or join screen? (open-ended)

### Hosting

- Rate how clear it was to host a ride (1-5)
- Rate how easy it was to set your price (1-5)
- Was setting a price straightforward? (Yes/No)
- Would you want guidance on pricing? (Yes/No)
- Did you see where to add/edit your Venmo handle? (Yes/No)

### Payment & Pricing

- Rate your confidence about how payment would work, even without in-app payment (1-5)
- What would make payment info clearer? (open-ended)
- Would you prefer to pay before or after the ride? (Before/After + why)

### Notifications

- What notifications would you want from the app? (e.g., ride updates, payment reminders, messages, etc.)
- Did you receive any notifications during your tasks? (Yes/No)
- Rate how clear notification info was (1-5)
- Was anything about notifications confusing or missing? (open-ended)

### General Usability

- What was the most confusing part of your experience? (open-ended)
- If you could improve one thing, what would it be? (open-ended)
- Rate your confidence using this app for a real ride (1-5)
